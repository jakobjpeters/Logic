Markdown

PrettyTables.pretty_table — Method
pretty_table(
    ::Type{Markdown.MD}, ::Union{Proposition, TruthTable};
    formatters = v -> v ? "⊤" : "⊥", alignment = :l
)

Examples

julia> @atomize pretty_table(Markdown.MD, p ∧ q)
  p q p ∧ q
  – – –––––
  ⊤ ⊤ ⊤
  ⊥ ⊤ ⊥
  ⊤ ⊥ ⊥
  ⊥ ⊥ ⊥

julia> @atomize print(pretty_table(String, p ∧ q; backend = Val(:markdown)))
| p   | q   | p ∧ q |
|:--- |:--- |:----- |
| ⊤   | ⊤   | ⊤     |
| ⊥   | ⊤   | ⊥     |
| ⊤   | ⊥   | ⊥     |
| ⊥   | ⊥   | ⊥     |

PrettyTables.pretty_table — Method
pretty_table(
    ::LaTexify.LaTexString, x::Union{Proposition, TruthTable};
    backend = Val(:latex), kwargs...
)

Equivalent to LaTeXString(pretty_table(String, x; backend, kwargs...)).

Examples

julia> pretty_table(Latexify.LaTeXString, @atomize p ∧ q)
L"\begin{tabular}{|l|l|l|}
  \hline
  \textbf{$p$} & \textbf{$q$} & \textbf{$p \wedge q$} \\
  \texttt{Variable} & \texttt{Variable} & \texttt{Tree} \\\hline
  $\top$ & $\top$ & $\top$ \\
  $\bot$ & $\top$ & $\bot$ \\\hline
  $\top$ & $\bot$ & $\bot$ \\
  $\bot$ & $\bot$ & $\bot$ \\\hline
\end{tabular}
"
